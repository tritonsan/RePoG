# Fantasy Lens

Use when supernatural power, myth, impossible creatures, divine forces, or
enchanted places materially shape play.

## Decide

- Is the supernatural common, hidden, feared, regulated, sacred, or disputed?
- What is its source, and what can never produce it?
- What does power cost or risk?
- What resists, counters, interrupts, or exposes it?
- How does it reshape work, class, law, belief, travel, and ordinary life?
- Should scenes lean toward wonder, danger, or a named balance?

## Suggested World Material

Record one visible everyday consequence, one institution or group that
benefits, one group that pays a cost, and one misconception people hold. Give
every major power category a source, limit, risk, counter, and social meaning.

## Optional Mechanics To Offer

- `bounded_resources` for a genuinely bounded magical resource;
- `abilities_costs` for prerequisites, costs, cooldowns, or regeneration;
- `wounds` for curses, corruption, or supernatural injury;
- `clocks` for rituals, omens, or escalating magical pressure;
- `dice_resolution` when uncertain power use should be rolled.

Do not create mana, HP, spell slots, spell lists, classes, levels, or named
magic systems merely because this lens is selected.
