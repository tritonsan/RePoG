# Realistic Lens

Use when a real period, place, profession, institution, technology, medical
constraint, or social system must remain credible.

## Decide

- Which time, place, profession, culture, or institution needs grounding?
- Where may drama compress reality, and where must it not?
- Which laws, material limits, health risks, prices, distances, and travel
  times matter at the initial scale?
- What expertise is ordinary, trained, elite, illegal, or impossible?
- Which stereotypes, anachronisms, or false certainties must be avoided?

## Research Contract

Default to a Research Gate when a wrong fact could alter durable world truth,
character capability, travel, law, medicine, economy, or lived culture.
`needed_pending` cannot lock the playable world. If research is unavailable,
require explicit risk acceptance and bound it to the current scale.

## Optional Mechanics To Offer

- `strict_consumables` when exact supplies, money, or professional equipment
  create meaningful decisions;
- `wounds` when injury and recovery matter;
- `structured_travel` for real deadlines, schedules, or travel;
- `clocks` for investigations, pursuit, institutions, or legal pressure;
- `dice_resolution` when the table wants open uncertainty rather than GM judgment.

Do not mistake realism for maximum bookkeeping. Track only constraints that
can change a player decision or consequence.
