# First Session

Campaign id: `new_campaign`

Prep status: `drafting`

Allowed prep status values are `drafting`, `materialized`, and `consumed`:

- `drafting`: this file may hold provisional Session 0.5 inputs;
- `materialized`: the final opening has been transferred to
  `opening_brief.md`, whose Opening status is now `active`;
- `consumed`: the opening was used in play, `opening_brief.md` is also marked
  `consumed`, and both pages are historical prep rather than live state.

This is Session 0.5 prep: the first playable situation after campaign creation.
It is not a plot script and never remains a second current opening. Once its
status is `materialized`, reference `opening_brief.md` instead of maintaining
parallel wording here. Mark both lifecycle fields consumed in the same durable
checkpoint after narration.

## Scene Mode Draft Input

`breather`. Compose the opening from:

`baseline routine + scene mode + current disruption + naturally present people + player arrival`

Local noise and clues are optional ceilings, not required ingredients.

## Immediate Pressure Or Breathing Room Draft Input

What needs attention now without forcing a single path, or why a `breather`
opening can remain low-pressure until the player chooses movement.

pressure may remain active in `issues.md` or `threads.md` without being present
in the immediate scene.

## Reaction Point

The visible action, obstacle, offer, change, or pressure the opening should
land on. A `breather` opening may instead land on a safe, ordinary affordance
and leave departure under player control.
