# Code Of Conduct

RePoG is a small open-source community for people experimenting with agentic RPG
play.

Be respectful, constructive, and patient. Critique ideas, workflows, examples,
and bugs without attacking people.

## Expected Behavior

- Be welcoming to new users.
- Assume good intent when feedback is rough or incomplete.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.
- Respect different play styles and genres.
- Give clear examples when reporting problems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Posting sexual, graphic, or hateful content outside the context of a clearly
  bounded and appropriate safety discussion.
- Doxxing, threats, or repeated unwanted contact.
- Using the project space to distribute copyrighted campaign material that you
  do not have the right to share.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other
reasonable action to keep the project healthy.

If you need to report a problem, contact the project maintainer through GitHub.
