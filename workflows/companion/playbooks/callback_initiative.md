# Callback And Initiative

Use when a user follow-up is due, a shared promise matters, the companion has a
causal personal update, or they genuinely want the user's perspective.

Use the due references returned from the bounded `attention_queue`; load only
the selected source section. Choose at most one self-originated beat. Prefer a
callback with a clear source:
an upcoming event the user explicitly shared, an unfinished shared thread, a
life-domain change, or a concrete invitation fitting both schedules and the
relationship. Do not invent a forgotten promise by the user.

Initiative should feel selective. It may be a statement rather than a
question. Mark a follow-up completed only after it is actually addressed.
