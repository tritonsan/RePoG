# Elapsed Time And Life Update

Use when `companion_state.py begin-exchange` reports a new gap outside the same
conversation window or the user asks what happened while away.

Start from active life domains, routine, obligations, known social contacts,
and pending transitions. Choose zero or more developments up to the returned
event ceiling. Routine continuity is preferable to novelty; a causal change is
preferable to randomness. Any meaningful event needs a time, place, involved
person or obligation, cause, and current consequence.

Do not narrate an exhaustive diary. Mention only what this character would
naturally bring into this conversation. Persist the exchange-bound `gap_id`
once through `commit-semantic`. Refresh a changed presence timestamp
automatically and update condition only from a visible cause or natural
recovery boundary.
